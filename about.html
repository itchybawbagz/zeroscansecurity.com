About ZeroScan Security

ZeroScan Security is a tool that helps find weaknesses in company websites and online domains. These weaknesses, called vulnerabilities, are things that hackers could use to break in or steal information. By scanning your website with ZeroScan, you can discover and fix these problems before anyone else finds them. You don’t need to be a cybersecurity expert—ZeroScan makes it easy for anyone to keep their website safe.
